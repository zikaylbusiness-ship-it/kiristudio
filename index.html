Common Questions

Good Questions
Deserve Honest Answers.

How can one person deliver the work of five specialists? +
The same way a skilled contractor can build a house that would otherwise require a large crew: mastery of the full plan, combined with the best tools available. Kiri Studio uses professional-grade AI tools for video, music, and web production, which drastically reduce the time and cost of producing high-quality work. What used to require a full team and a large budget can now be delivered by one experienced director at a fraction of the price. Every peso you spend goes directly into your brand, not into office rent or management fees.
Is a real person involved, or does AI do everything? +
Think of AI the way you think of a camera or an editing suite. A camera does not make a great film on its own. A skilled director does. At Kiri Studio, every video, every piece of music, and every website is planned, directed, and refined by a real human being who understands your brand and your customers. The AI helps execute the work faster and at a quality that was previously only available to businesses with very large budgets. You get that quality at the price shown.
What if I only need one service, like a website or a single video? +
Kiri Studio works on monthly plans rather than one-off projects. The reason is simple: a great video pointing to a poor website, or a beautiful website with no visitors, will not grow your business. The value in what we offer comes from all five disciplines working together, consistently, over time. Clients who have tried hiring specialists one at a time typically spend more and see less, because nothing is coordinated. One plan. One director. One consistent result.
Who owns the videos, music, and website we create together? +
You do, completely and permanently. Once the work is delivered and paid for, your business holds full ownership of every video, every music track, every voice-over, and every line of website code we produce for you. You are free to use it, publish it, and keep it forever, with no ongoing licensing fees or strings attached.
